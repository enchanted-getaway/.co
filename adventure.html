UTTARAKHAND

RISHIKESH

Rishikesh offers thrilling adventure sports like white-water rafting, bungee jumping, kayaking, zip-lining, rock climbing, and trekking, making it a top destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

AULI

Auli is a picturesque hill station in Uttarakhand, India, known for its stunning views of the Himalayas and excellent skiing facilities, making it a popular destination for winter sports enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

NAINITAL

Nainital is a serene hill station in Uttarakhand, India, known for its beautiful Naini Lake, pleasant weather, and attractions like Naina Devi Temple and Snow View Point, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking a peaceful retreat.

JIM CORBETT NATIONAL PARK

Jim Corbett National Park in Uttarakhand, India, is known for its rich biodiversity and offers wildlife safaris, birdwatching, and nature walks, making it a must-visit for nature enthusiasts and wildlife lovers.


HARIDWAR

Haridwar, in Uttarakhand, India, is a sacred city along the Ganges River, renowned for its religious festivals, spiritual rituals like Ganga Aarti, and ancient temples like Har Ki Pauri, making it a significant pilgrimage destination for Hindus.

MUSSORIE

Mussoorie is a scenic hill station in Uttarakhand, India, known for its beautiful landscapes, pleasant weather, colonial architecture, and attractions like Gun Hill and Kempty Falls, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking a peaceful retreat.

GOA

CALAUNGATUS BEACH

Calangute Beach in North Goa, India, is famous for its golden sands, clear waters, water sports, beachside shacks, and vibrant nightlife, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking sun, sand, and entertainment.

ANJUNA BEACH

Anjuna Beach in North Goa, India, is known for its scenic beauty, lively atmosphere, vibrant flea market, music scene, and water sports, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking entertainment and beach activities.

PALOLEM BEACH

Palolem Beach is a beautiful crescent-shaped beach located in South Goa, India, known for its tranquil atmosphere and scenic beauty. It is famous for its clear turquoise waters, soft white sands, and palm-fringed shoreline, making it a paradise .

AGUADA FORT

Houses over 100 well-preserved vintage cars, including iconic brands like Rolls-Royce, Bentley, and Mercedes-Benz.

SALIM ALI BIRD SANCTUARY

HIMACHAL PRADESH

MANALI

Manali, Himachal Pradesh,: Summer offers trekking, paragliding, and rafting; winter brings skiing and snow activities.

SHIMLA

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh, India: Colonial architecture, outdoor activities like trekking, skiing, ice skating in winter; year-round scenic destination.

SPITI VALLEY

piti Valley in Himachal Pradesh, India: remote, stunning landscapes, ancient monasteries, unique culture, attracts adventurous travelers.

DHARAM SHALA

Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh, India: Tibetan culture, monasteries, Dalai Lama, mountain views, trekking, attracts diverse travelers.

KULLU

Kullu, Himachal Pradesh, India: Scenic valley, Beas River, Dussehra festival, adventure activities, ancient temples, , attracts diverse visitors.

KASOL

Kasol, Himachal Pradesh, India: Serene village, Parvati Valley, scenic beauty, lush landscapes, tranquil ambiance, popular tourist spot.

ANDAMAN AND NICOBAR ISLANDS

HAVELOCK ISLAND

Havelock Island is a beautiful destination in India. It is renowned for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs.

NEIL ISLAND

Neil Island, Andaman and Nicobar Islands: Pristine beaches, coral reefs, tranquil ambiance, ideal for nature lovers, snorkeling, diving, picturesque spots.

BARATANG LIMESTONE CAVES

The Baratang Limestone Caves are a natural attraction in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, India, known for their unique limestone formations and geological beauty.

PORT BLAIR

Port Blair, Andaman and Nicobar Islands, India: Capital city, rich history , stunning beaches , water sports , Anthropological Museum, gateway for exploring nearby islands, offers history, nature, and adventure.

ROSS ISLAND

Ross Island, Andaman and Nicobar Islands: Historic island, former British administrative headquarters, colonial ruins, picturesque scenery, small museum.

NORTH BAY ISLAND

North Bay Island, Andaman and Nicobar Islands, India: Picturesque island, stunning coral reefs, diverse marine life, water sports activities, accessible by ferry from Port Blair, popular day-trip destination.

RAJASTHAN

JAIPUR

This iconic monument, built by the Qutub Shahi Kings, features an impressive structure with eight gates and 87 bastions. Don’t miss the light and sound show narrated by Bollywood superstar Amitabh.

JAISALMER

Jaisalmer, Rajasthan, India: Mesmerizing city in Thar Desert, stunning sandstone architecture, Jaisalmer Fort, "Golden City," camel safaris, cultural heritage, must-visit for history and desert experience.

UDAIPUR

Udaipur, Rajasthan, India: "City of Lakes," majestic palaces (City Palace, Lake Palace), vibrant markets, cultural performances, royal charm, must-visit for history, culture, and natural beauty.

RANTHAMBORE NATIONAL PARK

Ranthambore National Park, Rajasthan, India: Renowned wildlife sanctuary, tigers, diverse flora and fauna, popular for safaris and wildlife photography.

MOUNT ABU

Mount Abu, Rajasthan, India: Picturesque hill station, Aravalli Range, only hill station in Rajasthan, famous for its scenic beauty, Nakki Lake, Dilwara Temples, popular tourist destination.

PUSHKAR

Pushkar, Rajasthan, India: Sacred town, Pushkar Lake, Brahma Temple, vibrant culture, spiritual significance.

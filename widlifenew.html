    Kaziranga National Park

    Kaziranga National Park famous for its one-horned Rhinoceros is located in Golaghat, Nagaon and Sonitpur districts of Assam.

    Manas National Park is also known as Manas Wildlife Sanctuary. It is famous for Tigers and Tiger conservation.

    Gir National Park

    Gir National Park in Gujarat is famous for Asiatic lions. It is also known as Sasan Gir .

    JAMMU AND KASHMIR

    Hemis National Park

    Hemis National Park is a high-elevation national park in Ladakh, India. Globally famous for its snow leopards.

    KERALA

    Periyar National Park

    Periyar National Park is famous for its wildlife and is home to a variety of species, including elephants, tigers, leopards and a variety of bird species.

    MADHYA PRADESH

    Kanha National Park

    Kanha National Park park has a significant population of the Royal Bengal tiger, Indian leopards, the sloth bear, barasingha and Indian wild dog.

    Bandhavgarh National Park

    Bandhavgarh national park is one of the famed tiger abodes of India, sheltering Worlds highest density of Bengal Tigers.

    Pench National Park

    It is famous for its ferocious Bengal Tigers that boasts a significant amount of tourism in the park.

    Satpura National Park

    Indian Giant squirrel is one of the special feature of Satpura Tiger Reserve as it is rare in India now.

    MAHARASHTRA

    Tadoba National Park

    The main attraction of the Tadoba National Park is Jungle or Tiger Safari in an open top gypsy.

    RAJISTHAN

    Ranthambore National Park

    Ranthambore is known for its Bengal tiger population. During the past few years, there has been a decline in numbers due to poaching and other reasons.

    UTTARAKHAND

    Jim Corbett National Park

    Jim Corbett National Park is a forested wildlife sanctuary in northern India’s Uttarakhand State.

    WEST BENGAL

    Sundarban National Park

    The Sundarbans, vital for endangered species like Royal Bengal Tigers, Ganges & Irawadi dolphins, and estuarine crocodiles. It s the sole mangrove habitat for the Panthera tigris tigris species.
